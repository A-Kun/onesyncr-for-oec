Team's Agreement:

  • We communicate using WeChat.
  • Individual message and @mentions in group chat should be responded as soon as a member sees it. Other messages are optional general discussions. (But who doesn’t want to join our nerdy buy friendly discussions?)
  • We meet weekly at the tutorial, and discussion happens after the tutorial.
  • Meetings are mostly held online when new tasks are posted or when required.
  • Meeting minutes are taken by everyone, collaborating in a Google Doc document.
  • Every team member should read newly posted handouts before a meeting commences.
  • Git commit messages should be clear and meaningful.
  • Everyone can contribute to the Git repo, but need to let everybody know what you are working on to avoid conflicts.
  • Work division will be done on a voluntary basis; everybody can claim what they want to do.
  • We don’t want to see any team member drops out or gets sick. So please work hard and get some exercises. However, if this happens unfortunately, a team meeting is required and all work belonging to the leaving member should be reassigned.
